Cloud-to-Cloud (Back-End Data-Sharing) Communication Model

This type of communication model extends the device-to-cloud communication type such that the data from the IoT devices can be accessed by authorized third parties. Here, devices upload their data onto the cloud, which is later accessed or analyzed by third parties. An example of this model would be an analyzer of the yearly or monthly energy consumption of a company.

Physical security controls are categorized into:

  • Prevention Controls: These are used to prevent unwanted or unauthorized access to resources. They include access controls such as fences, locks, biometrics, and mantraps.
  • Deterrence Controls: These are used to discourage the violation of security policies. They include access controls such as security guards and warning signs.
  • Detection Controls: These are used to detect unauthorized access attempts. They include access controls such as CCTV and alarms.
